I'm vampire, and you?

First project

We want to introduce you our game called "I'm vampire, and you?". So in this game you can find parts from different types of games like Survival: you can craft, collect some stuff, complete quests, survive in the world full of vampires.

What about the story? You are teen, who lived in time when the plague became. You must survive. You're living with your parents and they can check, vampire you or not. You should alive much time as you can and you can do the tasks from your parents and craft some useful stuff like antidote.

You won't regret about your time which will be spended on our game because you can be involved in the wonderful world full of monsters who want kill you. Don't miss our offer to ask the main question: "Are you a vampire?"
