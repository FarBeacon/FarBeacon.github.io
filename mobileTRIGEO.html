TRIGEO

  • Our first mobile game

Go around the shapes and try to fly as far as possible in this stylish endless runner. Do you often miss when holding your phone? This anti-stress game will help you get distracted! There is no shooting and incredible action in it, because you are already tired of all this! Take on the role of the hero - the war of infinity, for which the game is the whole life!

TRIGEO - super dynamic arcade game on android, where you will travel the world of shapes. In this game, your main task will be to go as far as possible and at the same time you will not encounter various and numerous figures. Go around all sorts of shapes and play this game in any free time.

The game will give you excellent bright graphics, relaxing gameplay, simple and intuitive controls. Touch the screen of your phone or tablet at the right time to turn to the side, and make your way through a hostile world. Hold on as long as possible in the game and set your record!

• Relaxing, simple gameplay
• Stylish HD graphics
• Sensor or tilt control
• A variety of skins
• Russian and English interface
• Wonderful music
